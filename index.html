Marvel Fight Calculator

Type your Marvel characters, and this will bring up official feats and official stats to see who would win.

Things to note:

1.) If it's not in the search bar, then that character isn't added yet.
2.) Duplicates won't do anything.
3.) This is comic book universe NOT MCU.
4.) These stats are from the official handbook of the Marvel Universe.
5.) These are from 616 universe, unless otherwise stated.
